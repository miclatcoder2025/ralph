About Us

M&M Lettuce Farm and Samgy Garden is built on a foundation of quality, sustainability, and a love for nature. Below, you’ll find our mission, vision, core values, and the unique experience we offer.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality produce and authentic samgyupsal experiences straight from our farm to your table. We aim to create a sanctuary where families and friends can bond, dine, and appreciate the beauty of nature’s gifts.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as one of the leading farm-to-table destinations in the country — promoting sustainable farming, inspiring healthier lifestyles, and bringing communities closer to nature through good food and great experiences.

Our Core Values

At M&M Lettuce Farm and Samgy Garden, we value sustainability, authenticity, family, and community. Every dish we serve and every vegetable we grow reflects our deep commitment to care, quality, and love for nature.

The Experience

Beyond just dining, we offer an immersive farm experience. Walk through our gardens, see where your food is grown, and feel the peaceful embrace of nature — creating memories that last beyond the meal.
