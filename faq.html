Frequently Asked Questions

(M&M Lettuce Farm and Samgy Garden)

1. What are your resort's operating hours?

Our beautiful resort operates daily. The pool area is open from 9:00 AM onwards, perfect for early morning swims and family bonding. Meanwhile, our Samgy Garden restaurant welcomes guests starting 11:00 AM until 11:00 PM, giving you plenty of time to enjoy unlimited Samgyupsal and other delicious dishes in a relaxing farm setting.

2. Do you serve Samgyupsal all day?

Yes, definitely! Our Samgy Garden is proud to serve authentic unlimited Samgyupsal from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Whether you’re dropping by for lunch, a mid-afternoon feast, or a late-night craving, we are ready to grill with you! Fresh meats, side dishes, and veggies from our own lettuce farm are always available.

3. Can we walk in without a reservation?

Walk-ins are welcome at M&M Lettuce Farm and Samgy Garden! However, due to high demand especially during weekends and holidays, we highly encourage guests to book ahead to secure a table or a room. Reservations also help us prepare better to give you a smoother and more enjoyable experience.

4. Are pets allowed inside the resort?

Absolutely! Our resort is pet-friendly because we believe that pets are family too. You can bring your fur babies with you, but we kindly request all owners to keep them on a leash at all times and to clean up after them to maintain the cleanliness and peaceful vibe of the farm.

5. Do you have private rooms or cottages?

Yes, we offer a variety of accommodations to match your needs. Choose from private rooms, garden villas, dormitory-style rooms, and cozy cottages. Whether you’re staying for just the day or planning an overnight escape, we have the perfect space for you to relax and recharge.

6. What amenities are available?

We’re more than just a restaurant! M&M Lettuce Farm and Samgy Garden offers a wide range of amenities including a swimming pool, Samgyupsal restaurant, private villas, open garden spaces, karaoke rooms for singing your heart out, and beautiful farm landscapes perfect for photoshoots and events.

7. How do we make a reservation?

Booking a stay or a table is easy! You can reserve by visiting our official website, messaging us through our Facebook page, or contacting us directly through our provided mobile numbers. Our team is happy to assist you with your inquiries and confirm your bookings as soon as possible.

8. Can we bring our own food and drinks?

To ensure food safety and to maintain the quality of our dining experience, outside food and drinks are not allowed. But no worries — we offer unlimited Samgyupsal, rice meals, ala carte dishes, and refreshing drinks that will surely satisfy your cravings during your stay.

9. Is there parking available?

Yes! We have a spacious and free parking area inside the resort grounds. Whether you’re coming in a private car, van, or even a small bus for group outings, you’ll have a safe and convenient place to park while you enjoy your visit.
